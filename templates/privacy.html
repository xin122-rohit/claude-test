Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

We collect the information you provide when creating an account (name, email address, password) and the expense data you enter into the app (amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions). We do not collect payment information or any financial account credentials.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Spendly service — displaying your expense history, generating summaries, and enabling you to manage your records. We do not use your financial data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not sell or share your personal data with third parties. We may use essential infrastructure services (hosting, database) to operate the app, but these providers are bound by confidentiality obligations and are not permitted to use your data independently.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us at privacy@spendly.app. We will respond to all inquiries within 7 business days.
